# Change Words 1

Now, we want to change words using 3 simple vim motions:

- `cw` - change word - change from the cursor to the end of the word
- `caw` - change around word - change around the word, including the trailing white space
- `ciw` - change in word - change the inside word, excluding the white space

- `dw` - delete word - deletes from the cursor to the end of the word.
- `daw` - delete around word - deletes the entire word, including any trailing whitespace.
- `diw` - delete inner word - deletes the word under the cursor, but does not include any surrounding whitespace.

- `vw` - visual word - visually selects from the cursor to the end of the word.
- `vaw` - visual around word - visually selects the entire word and any surrounding whitespace.
- `viw` - visual inner word - visually selects the word under the cursor, but does not include any surrounding whitespace.
